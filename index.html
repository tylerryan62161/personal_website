About

We will explore the three eras of the Cincinnati Bengals football team, compiling historical and current information in a single, convenient location. The first era will include the origins of the team and its founder, Paul Brown. The second era will focus on the three decades after Paul gave control of the team to his son, Mike Brown. Finally, we will delve into the current state of the team during its transition from Mike to his daughter, Katie Blackburn, as well as its outlook moving forward.

PAST

Paul Brown - Godfather of Football

Innovations:

  • Created the playbook
  • First to use 40 yard dash time to evaluate functional speed
  • First to value speed over strength
  • Invented the draw play - a delayed handoff to the running back
  • Created the pocket - area behind the offensive line for QBs to throw
  • Helped create the West Coast offense - most common offense used today
  • First to hire assistant coaches full time
  • First to designate assistant coaches to each position
  • First to teach schemes in classroom setting and test for retension
  • First to have strict organization of practices
  • First to analyze game film to make improvements or identify opponents' weaknesses
  • First to create detailed system to scout players and improve team drafts
  • First to utilize a practice squad - fringe players to develop
  • First to call plays to the QB using hand signals
  • Integral in developing the face mask
  • First to test and value intelligence in his players

Accomplishments:

  • Found success as a player while being outclassed athletically
  • Revived and made dominant his high school football program as a coach
  • Took Ohio State to new heights
  • Did his duty as an American citizen, joining the Navy and giving up his esteemed position at Ohio State
  • Dominated the AAFC so badly that interest waned from lack of competition
  • Entered the NFL with the same team and continued his domination
  • Only person to help found 2 separate NFL franchises
  • Most importantly, pioneered integrating black players into the league

PRESENT

Mike Brown - Fall From Grace

Career Statistics

  • Seven winning seasons in 30 years as owner/GM
  • Took 15 years to produce his first winning season
  • In the seven years that his teams made the playoffs, they immediately lost in the first round
  • Secured the longest playoff victory drought of 30 years
  • Slowest owner to win 100 games ever
  • Quickest owner to lose 200 games ever
  • Personally holds 24% of the total 0-6 season starts in the NFL
  • Personally holds 31% of the total 0-8 season starts in the NFL

FUTURE

Katie Blackburn - Heir Apparent

Accomplishments/Changes

  • First female chief contract negotiator in the NFL
  • On track to be the 5th female president in the NFL
  • Consensus based decision making rather than thinking she always knows best
  • Member of Workplace Diversity Committee
  • Chair of Super Bowl Advisory Committee
  • Most successful five year stretch in franchise history immediately after she was given control
  • Willingness to move on from underperforming coaches/players
  • Revamped social media presence
  • Finally updating jerseys after two decades
  • Celebrating greatest past members - hinting at ring of honor
  • Lets head coach assume full control over coaching staff and players
  • Willing to spend on ancillary aspects of the team
  • Willing to spend in free agency
